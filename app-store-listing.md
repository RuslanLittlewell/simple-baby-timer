## Description

Simple Baby Tracker helps you follow your baby's daily rhythm without any fuss. Tap once to start a timer for sleep, feeding or awake time — and the app quietly keeps the record for you.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• One‑tap timers for Sleep, Feeding and Awake time
• Smart reminders based on your baby's own schedule:
   – "Still sleeping?" when a nap runs long
   – "Still awake?" when it's time to wind down
• Feeding auto‑stop — the feeding timer ends itself and notifies you
• Day timeline calendar — see the whole day at a glance, color‑coded by activity
• Pinch to zoom the timeline from a full‑day overview down to the minute
• Edit any entry and log the amount of milk for feedings
• Daily stats — total sleep, awake time and milk at a glance
• Floating mini‑timer that follows you across the app while a timer runs
• Personalize sleep, awake and feeding durations in Settings

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
All data stays on your device. No account, no sign‑up, no ads, works fully offline.

SIMPLE ON PURPOSE
No clutter, no endless charts — just the three things that matter most in the early months.
